1. Description of the App

Prop Lot Size is a position-size calculator for prop-firm and forex traders. You enter your account balance, risk, stop distance, and instrument, and the App computes a lot size. When you select a prop firm and account configuration, the App also checks the trade against that configuration's daily-loss and maximum-drawdown limits and shows whether it fits, is close to the limit, or would breach it. Firm rules are a starting point, not a guarantee. Prop firms change their rules frequently and some let you configure limits at purchase. The App ships researched rule sets with the date they were last reviewed, and lets you enter your own limits, which always take precedence over the bundled values. Always confirm your actual limits with your firm — the App's breach check is only as accurate as the rule set you select or enter.
