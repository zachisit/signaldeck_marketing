Know Exactly Where Your Fills Are Costing You.

Day traders live and die by execution. SignalDeck measures it on every single trade.

Entry Slippage

The difference between your intended entry and your actual fill. Identifies whether your market orders are costing you edge at open.

Exit Slippage

How much you're giving back on exit. High exit slippage on winning trades means your actual R is lower than planned.

MAE (Adverse Excursion)

How far a trade moved against you before turning profitable. Reveals whether your stops are too tight or your entries too early.

MFE (Favorable Excursion)

The maximum profit available on each trade before you exited. If MFE consistently exceeds your actual exit, you're leaving R on the table.
