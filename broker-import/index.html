Trading Journal with Broker Import:
What to Know Before You Choose.

Broker auto-import is convenient. It's also the fastest way to build a journal full of data you can't use to calculate your actual edge. Here's the honest breakdown of what broker import gives you — and what it doesn't.

⚡

SignalDeck does not have broker auto-import.

SignalDeck requires manual entry or CSV import. We made this tradeoff deliberately — if you need 700+ broker integrations today, TraderSync is the right tool. Broker API integrations are on our roadmap. What SignalDeck chose to build instead is documented below.

Auto-Import vs Manual Logging: The Real Tradeoff

Feature Broker Auto-Import Manual / CSV Entry
Time to log a trade Automatic 2–3 min
P&L and execution data ✓ ✓
Stop loss captured Rarely ✓ (required)
R-Multiple calculable No stop = no R ✓
Trade rationale / thesis Not captured ✓
Psychological context Not captured ✓ (behavioral tags)
Forced pre-trade discipline review None Every trade
SQN calculable Requires R (see above) ✓

The root issue: R-Multiple requires a stop loss. Broker executions don't include the stop you had in your head — only the fills that hit. Without a stop, there's no R. Without R, there's no expectancy, no SQN, no Kelly Criterion. Auto-import builds a large dataset fast. It's often a dataset you can't use for the metrics that actually predict long-run profitability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SignalDeck have broker auto-import?

No. SignalDeck uses manual entry or CSV import. Broker API integrations are on the roadmap. If auto-import is your primary requirement today, TraderSync and Tradervue are better fits.

Which trading journals have broker auto-import?

TraderSync (700+ brokers), Tradervue (80+ brokers), and TradesViz all support broker auto-import. TraderSync is the market leader for this feature.

Why doesn't broker import support R-Multiple tracking?

R-Multiple requires your initial stop loss. Broker executions capture fills and P&L — not the stop you planned before entry. Without the stop, the R calculation is impossible. Manual logging solves this by requiring the stop at the time of entry.
