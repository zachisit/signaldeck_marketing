What You Get

Access you can't buy anywhere else.

Every major trading journal has a support portal, a help center, or a community forum. None of them give you what SignalDeck gives you for free during beta.

1:1 Onboarding Call

Every new beta tester gets a personal walkthrough. We help you import your existing trades, set up your risk profile, configure your first strategies, and walk through the Post Mortem protocol. No self-service docs maze — a real conversation with a real human who knows every corner of the product.

Direct Voice to the Founder

Ping the founder in #general or DM. We read every message. This is a small community by design — you'll never be talking to a community manager or a support tier 1, you'll be talking to the people writing the code. Weekly #founder-office-hours for live Q&A.

Feature Requests That Actually Ship

Request a feature in #feedback and watch it ship — often within 48 hours. This isn't lip service. Check the release history — most updates came from direct Discord conversations. When the founder is also the product manager, the feedback loop is a few hours, not a few quarters.

Same-Day Support Triage

Bug? Broken chart? Confused by a metric? Post in #support and get an answer fast. Most issues triaged the same day. Critical bugs escalated immediately. No ticket queue, no canned responses, no "your request is important to us" boilerplate.

Why Small

We're intentionally small.
That's the whole point.

Other trading journals have Discord servers with 20,000+ members, where a feature request gets lost in the noise and the founder hasn't posted in six months. We're the opposite. SignalDeck is a small community by design — currently under 100 active traders — because that's how we can give you this level of access. The founder knows everyone's name. Every feature in the release history came from a Discord conversation.

When we scale, the early members keep Founder Status forever — free for life on any plan, and first-class access to the founder when everyone else has to go through a support portal.
