The Mission

We are building a forensic audit layer for retail traders. We're in stealth mode, pivoting our engine to help traders identify "Strategic Drift" and discipline errors before they blow their accounts. We're currently working on a feature to layer forex on top of our existing interface. Seeking a team member to help with this launch. You'll be working directly with the founder to QA the forex-specific feature set and build the outreach engine to get this in front of traders across the entire forex ecosystem.

What You Will Do

  • GTM Research & Mapping: You'll map out the forex landscape on X (Twitter), Instagram, and LinkedIn. Your job is to source leads across the board - from small independent traders and prop firm participants to up and coming forex social media influencers.
  • Lead Sourcing & Scraping: You will be responsible for building out our database of contacts. You'll find the active participants in the forex conversation and categorize them for outreach.
  • Human-to-Human Outreach: You'll draft personalized, non-salesy messages to start conversations with these leads. No bots, no templates - just human outreach written like a peer.
  • Forex Product QA: You'll help test the app's logic against forex market scenarios. You'll look at things like pip calculations, swap rates, and leverage impacts to make sure the "audit" makes sense for a forex user.
  • Founder Collaboration: You'll work directly with the founder for all technical and strategy syncs. You'll see the "under the hood" logic of a stealth startup pivot.

Who You Are

  • Forex-Literate: You know the basics and then some. You understand pips, lot sizes, leverage, swap rates, and you stay updated on the general macroeconomic narrative driving currency pairs.
  • GTM Desired: You want to learn how to launch a product with zero marketing budget. You're interested in the mechanics of growth: scraping, sourcing, and ghostwriting.
  • High Agency: You don't need a 100-step manual. You're comfortable with a "figure it out" environment and taking ownership of your lead lists.
  • Detail Oriented: You can spot a logic error in a feature or a typo in a message from a mile away.

What You Get

  • The War Room Experience: A front-row seat to a forex pivot. You'll learn how to build an outreach engine that actually converts without sounding like a bot.
  • Technical Mentorship: Direct insight into how a forensic AI layer is built using Gemini 1.5 Flash and institutional metrics.
