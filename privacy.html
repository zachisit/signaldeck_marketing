Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 21, 2026

SignalDeck ("SignalDeck", "we", "us", or "our") operates the website at sgnldk.com and the application at app.sgnldk.com (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the Service you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for an account we collect your email address and a hashed password. If you sign up via Google OAuth we receive your name and email address from Google.

Trading Data You Provide

The core purpose of SignalDeck is to help you record and analyze your trades. Data you enter or import — including trade records, journal entries, strategy configurations, post-mortem notes, and account balances — is stored on our servers and is yours. We do not sell this data.

Broker Connection Data

If you connect a broker account (e.g., via MT4/MT5 import or other integrations), we store the OAuth tokens and connection metadata required to sync your trade history. These credentials are encrypted at rest and used solely to retrieve your trade data on your behalf.

Usage and Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how users interact with the marketing site. GA4 collects anonymized data including pages visited, session duration, general geographic region, and device type. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Log and Technical Data

Our servers automatically collect standard log data when you access the Service, including IP address, browser type, referring URLs, and timestamps. This data is used for security monitoring and debugging and is not shared with third parties except as described below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To sync your trading data with connected broker accounts
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts)
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve product features
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your trading data to train machine learning models, sell to data brokers, or share with any third party for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited set of third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized marketing site analytics
  • Stripe — payment processing for paid plans; we do not store card numbers
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — cloud infrastructure and data storage; data is stored in US-based regions
  • Mailjet — transactional email delivery
  • MetaAPI / broker integration providers — used to sync trade data from MT4/MT5 accounts at your direction

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data required for each provider to perform its function.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and trade records within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate security purposes (e.g., fraud prevention logs).

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive credentials, hashed passwords, and access controls limiting who on our team can view user data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — request that your account and associated data be deleted
  • Portability — request an export of your trading data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, reach out via our contact page.

7. Cookies

The marketing site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to distinguish unique sessions. The application uses session cookies for authentication. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify active users via email. Continued use of the Service after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via our contact page or connect with us on LinkedIn.
