FAQ

What is a prop firm profit split?

The profit split is the share of profits you keep. Most firms pay the trader 80–90%; some advertise up to 100% within a capped payout ladder. Enter your profit and pick a firm (or a custom split) to see your take-home.

Does this account for payout caps or fees?

No — this is the straight split on a given profit amount. Real payouts can be capped per cycle, require a minimum number of winning days, or carry activation fees. Always confirm the cadence and caps with your firm.
