Frequently Asked Questions

Does SignalDeck have broker auto-import?

Yes — SignalDeck supports MT4 and MT5 trade import via MetaApi. Connect your account and trades import automatically (fills, P&L, symbol, volume, price). Live account balance sync is also included. Manual entry and CSV import remain available for non-MT4/MT5 platforms.

Does MT4/MT5 import capture the stop loss for R-Multiple?

No — the MetaApi history-deals endpoint returns execution data (fills) but not the stop loss from the original order. Stop loss still needs to be added manually after import for R-Multiple to be calculable. The import saves time on execution data; R-Multiple requires the post-trade review step where you add your planned stop.

What is live account balance sync and why does it matter for position sizing?

SignalDeck syncs your MT4/MT5 account balance in real time. Your Kelly Criterion recommendation and fixed-R risk sizing always reflect your actual current equity — not a number you typed in last month. After a drawdown, your sizing adjusts automatically. This is something no journal that relies on manual balance entry can replicate.
