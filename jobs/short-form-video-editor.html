Short-Form Video Editor

📍 Remote 💼 Unpaid 🎬 Video & Content 🗓 1–3 Months

"We turn daily live trading sessions into YouTube Shorts — and short-form is our single biggest growth lever. We need an editor who's genuinely obsessed with the first 1.5 seconds, not just clean cuts."

The Role

@ForensicQuant is a fast-growing trading-content channel behind a fintech startup. We livestream real trading and market analysis every weekday and repurpose the best moments into short-form video. You'll own the cut: take each day's stream + a list of flagged clips and turn them into Shorts that actually retain and get distributed. This is a daily-cadence, in-the-weeds creative role — perfect if you live and breathe hooks and retention curves.

What You'll Be Doing

  • Daily Shorts: Turn the day's livestream + flagged clips into 2–3 polished vertical Shorts (1080×1920).
  • Hooks & Retention: Nail the first 1.5 seconds, add clean captions, keep pacing tight — optimize for watch-through, not just looks.
  • Formats & Series: Work our recurring series and pitch titles + which clips will pop.
  • Reliable Cadence: We publish ~2/day, 7 days a week — consistent daily turnaround matters more than perfection.

Who You Are

  • Fluent in short-form editing (CapCut / Premiere / DaVinci) and, above all, hook-writing.
  • Ideally markets-literate so the hooks land with traders — not required if you learn fast.
  • Fast, reliable, detail-oriented and comfortable with a daily rhythm.
  • On-brand by instinct: educational and analytical — no hype, no "get rich" energy, no financial advice. Credibility is the brand.

Why Join Us?

  • Direct Founder Access: Work straight with the founder; your edits drive the company's #1 growth channel.
  • Real Ownership: Test formats, see the retention data, double down on what breaks out.
  • A Clear Portfolio: Daily published work + measurable channel growth you can point to.

Compensation & Term

Compensation: Unpaid Term: 1, 2, or 3 months · ~1–2 hrs/day

Want in?

Send 2–3 short-form examples you edited (trading/finance a plus) and the hook you'd open a trading clip with.
