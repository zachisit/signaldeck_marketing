Social Media Manager

📍 Remote 💼 Unpaid 📣 Social Media & Content 🗓 1–3 Months

"Walk into your next job interview with the ability to confidently say: I worked with a lean startup to generate their first MRR — proving a custom content strategy executing across multiple social media platforms targeted to their ICP. Worked 1:1 with the founder to generate content that created high conversions. I was in the weeds daily, and my efforts produced direct value to the startup when it needed it most: pre-seed."

The Role

We are looking for a scrappy, hands-on social media hustler to help us build our digital presence from the ground up. You won't be managing an agency or handing off ideas to a massive design team — you will be the one in the trenches making it happen. If you thrive in the chaos of a 0-to-1 environment and want to see the direct revenue impact of your daily posts, this is your playground.

What You'll Be Doing

  • Content Creation: Write, design, and produce engaging text, image, and short-form video content specifically tailored to our Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
  • Platform Ownership: Run the daily execution across our key channels (X/Twitter) — adapting the message to fit the medium.
  • Founder Collaboration: Work directly 1:1 with the founder to extract the company vision and translate it into high-converting social campaigns.
  • Community Engagement: Jump into the comments, spark conversations with industry peers, and build our early audience organically.
  • Testing & Iterating: Throw ideas at the wall, track the analytics, double down on what drives conversions, and ruthlessly cut what doesn't.

Who You Are

  • A Builder at Heart: You love the pre-seed phase and aren't afraid of ambiguity or building the playbook as you go.
  • Action-Oriented: You prefer hitting "publish" and iterating over spending three weeks drafting the perfect strategy deck.
  • Deeply Resourceful: You know how to make a big impact with a small budget using native platform tools and a lot of creativity.
  • Results-Driven: You care more about generating actual pipeline and Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) than you do about vanity metrics.

Why Join Us?

  • Direct Founder Access: A massive voice in the company's early direction and a direct line to the person making all the calls.
  • Total Autonomy: Freedom to test your wildest content ideas without layers of approval slowing you down.
  • An Undeniable Case Study: The opportunity to build proof-of-work out of your own success — first MRR, first conversions, yours.

Compensation & Term

Compensation: Unpaid Term: 1, 2, or 3 months depending on fit
